# CanDash Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [PUBLISH_DATE]

CanDash ("the app") is developed and published by SwiftCarTech ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app handles and how. We have written it to be as plain and short as possible.

## Summary

CanDash does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app stores stays on your device. We do not run analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

## What the app does on your device

CanDash connects to an OBD-II adapter in your vehicle to read live diagnostic data (engine RPM, speed, coolant temperature, fault codes, and similar). To do this, it needs access to one or more of the following on your device:

- **Bluetooth** — to talk to wireless OBD-II adapters (Veepeak, OBDLink CX, OBDLink MX+).
- **Location (while using the app)** — only used by the optional Performance tab to measure 0–60, ¼ mile and similar acceleration runs using GPS speed. Location data is read live during a run, displayed on screen, and discarded. It is never stored beyond the active run and never transmitted off your device.

## What is stored on your device

CanDash stores the following locally, on your device only:

- App preferences (gauge layout, refresh rate, units, night-mode setting, calibration offsets).
- Your saved custom OBD PIDs.
- Your data-logging trip recordings (CSV files in the app's Documents directory).
- The identifier of the last OBD adapter you connected to, so the app can reconnect automatically.
- Your in-app purchase status, restored from Apple when you launch the app.

You can delete this data at any time by deleting the app, or by clearing individual items from inside the app where the option is provided (for example, deleting saved trips in the Data Logging tab).

## What is NOT collected

We want to be explicit about what CanDash does **not** do:

- We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier.
- We do not require an account.
- We do not include any third-party analytics SDKs.
- We do not include advertising.
- We do not track your usage of the app.
- We do not send any vehicle data, location data, or diagnostic data to our servers or any third party. We do not have servers.

## In-app purchases

CanDash offers optional one-time in-app purchases (CanDash Pro and vehicle-specific PID packs). All purchase processing is handled by Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment details. Apple's privacy policy applies to the purchase transaction itself: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

## Children

CanDash is not directed at children. It is a tool for adult vehicle owners. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

## Third parties

CanDash does not share data with third parties because it does not collect any data to share. The app communicates with two external systems only:

- **Your OBD-II adapter**, over Bluetooth or the iAP2 accessory protocol. Data flows from the vehicle to the app on your phone.
- **Apple's App Store**, for in-app purchases and purchase restoration.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected in the next app update's release notes.
